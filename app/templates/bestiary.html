How to Play

 The Journey

Elden Souls is a branching text adventure. Each chapter presents story choices that lead deeper into the world — some paths lead to battle, others to rest or lore. Read carefully; the choices you make shape the enemies you face.

 Battle Actions

Every turn you choose one action. The enemy always counters unless stunned.

Attack Deal damage based on your class attack stat. Crits deal ×1.5 damage. Builds 25 MP.
Dodge Attempt to evade the enemy's counter. Success depends on your class dodge chance (Rogue 70% → Knight 20%).
Block Absorb reduced damage. Each class blocks a different fraction (Knight takes only 25% of incoming damage).
Estus Flask Heal 70% of max HP. The enemy still counters. You start with 5 flasks (6 with Estus +1 gift). Restores at every bonfire.
Primary Special Costs 50 MP, shared cooldown. Unique per class — powerful burst, stun, or heal. Only Attack builds MP; blocking/dodging earns nothing.
Secondary Special Costs 35 MP, same shared cooldown. Each class has a second move with a different role — DoT, buff, shield, or sustain. Using either special puts both on cooldown.

 Primary Special Moves

Knight Shield Bash — Normal damage + stun. Enemy cannot counter this turn. 50 MP · 4-turn cooldown.
Mage Arcane Burst — 2× magic attack, bypasses all physical armour. 50 MP · 4-turn cooldown.
Rogue Smoke Screen — 1× attack damage + guaranteed dodge on the enemy's counter. 50 MP · 4-turn cooldown.
Archer Mark Target — Guaranteed 2× critical strike. 50 MP · 4-turn cooldown.
Paladin Healing Light — Restore 40% max HP + stun. Enemy cannot counter this turn. 50 MP · 5-turn cooldown.
Necromancer Raise the Dead — 2.5× magic attack with high variance. Undead assault tears through armour. 50 MP · 5-turn cooldown.

 Secondary Special Moves

Secondary specials cost 35 MP and share the same cooldown as the primary. Both buttons grey out when either fires. Choose based on the situation — burst vs sustained, offence vs defence.

Knight War Cry — No direct damage. Raises attack by +5 for 3 turns. Best when you have multiple attacks ahead and want sustained pressure.
Mage Nullfield — No damage. Reduces all incoming damage by 50% for 2 turns. The glass cannon's survival tool — use before a predicted big hit.
Rogue Backstab — 0.5× hit + bleed for 8 damage per turn for 4 turns (32 bleed total). Distinct from Smoke Screen — sets up sustained damage while you dodge safely.
Archer Poison Arrow — No hit damage. Poisons for 7 damage per turn for 5 turns (35 total). Matches Mark Target's total output at lower MP cost — better in long fights.
Paladin Hammer of Justice — 1.5× mixed damage + stun. Enemy cannot counter. Use when HP is healthy and healing would be wasteful — the Paladin's offensive option.
Necromancer Soul Leech — 1.5× magic damage, heals for 100% of damage dealt (minimum 15 HP). Sustain tool — use when hurt and the primary's raw damage isn't what you need.

 ATB Turn Timer

A 12-second timer counts down each turn. The bar shifts amber → orange → red as time runs low. If it expires, you take an unblocked penalty hit. Act before the bar empties.

 Bonfires

Rest chapters fully restore HP, Estus Flasks, and reset MP to 0. A merchant appears at bonfires — spend souls on permanent upgrades. Souls are not lost on death.

 Souls

Every enemy you kill drops souls. Bosses drop a 50% bonus on top. Spend them at the merchant for Attack Shards, Defense Shards, HP Vessels, or Estus Refills. The Old Coin starting gift begins the run with 200 souls.

 Boss Phases

Bosses enter Phase II when reduced to 50% HP. Move weights shift heavily toward big hits and flurries, and all damage increases by 20%. A unique lore message announces the transition. Plan your Estus and MP accordingly.

 Physical & Magic Damage

Every class and enemy has a damage type — ⚔ Physical or ✨ Magic. Physical attacks are reduced by the target's physical defence; magic attacks are reduced by their magic defence. Some bosses deal ⚔✨ Mixed damage, alternating between both types. Check the Enemy Codex to see what you're up against before you fight.

 Starting Gifts

  • Estus +1 — 6 Estus Flasks for the whole run.
  • Hunter's Charm — +5% critical hit chance.
  • Iron Talisman — +3 Defence.
  • Witch's Ember — +3 Attack.
  • Old Coin — Start with 200 souls.
  • Life Ring — +15 max HP.
  • Fading Soul — Nothing. For those who need no comfort.
